MEDICAL ROOM

We have an experienced doctor and a trained nurse to provide necessary first aid to the students in a time of emergency. An annual health check-up including dental and eye check-ups by specialists is a regular feature along with maintaining a medical record of each and every child. Health advisory on prevention of various illnesses like swine flu, dengue, chickenpox, etc. is also a regular feature.

Please Note

Students will be provided first aid only in the school premises in case of medical problem

In case of chronic diseases, school will only inform the parents telephonically. Parents will be expected to reach school at the earliest.

Expenses on first aid will be borne by the school. All other expenses in case of hospitalization/ visit to private clinic/ medicine will be borne by the parents.
